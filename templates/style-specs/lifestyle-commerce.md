# lifestyle-commerce Style Spec

## Position

Product-scene, catalog-proof, social/content commerce clarity. Use for FMCG, food and beverage, retail, creator content, live commerce, and social activation proposals.

## Color System

| Role | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Warm canvas | `#F7F2E8` | Main background |
| White panel | `#FFFFFF` | Product cards, screenshot frames, tables |
| Ink green | `#10231B` | Primary text |
| Soft green | `#66766B` | Captions and secondary copy |
| Retail green | `#0E6B4A` | Conversion path, product/category anchors |
| Tomato / coral | `#D86D55` | Platform emphasis, warning, hot item |
| Gold brown | `#A57B3E` | Price, premium cue, festival/gift node |
| Rule | `#DED5C6` | Card borders and table lines |

Rules:

- Warm, product-friendly, but not beige-only.
- Use platform colors only as labels; do not fake platform logos.
- Accent color must identify a channel, product category, or conversion step.

## Typography

| Layer | Font | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Main headings | PingFang SC / Source Han Sans / system sans | Bold, clear, sales-oriented |
| Product labels | PingFang SC / Arial | Compact, readable, tag-like |
| Body | PingFang SC | Short captions tied to conversion |
| Numbers | Inter / Arial | Sales, count, cost, CPE, budget, KPI |

Rules:

- Avoid luxury serif unless the category is premium gift or lifestyle.
- Product/category labels must remain readable in screenshot-like grids.

## Image And Asset Behavior

- Prefer product scenes, shelf/catalog images, package shots, creator screenshots, live-room stills, store/event images, product cutouts.
- Use consistent image ratios per page: 16:9 for scene, 1:1 for product tile, 9:16 for mobile screenshot.
- Each image needs a label: asset produced, channel role, conversion path, or proof value.
- Do not stack images randomly or let photos overlap without a clear editorial reason.

## Charts And Tables

- Platform matrix: channel, role, content action, asset, KPI, boundary.
- Calendar: campaign period, node, product, content format, owner.
- Budget: amount, deliverable, acceptance, exclusion.
- Funnel: awareness, search, trust, conversion, retention.
- Use clear row shading and category chips; no dense Excel grid.

## Module System

| Module | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Cover | Product scene or catalog hero + simple conversion thesis |
| Platform role | Three-column channel cards with image or screenshot proof |
| Content mechanism | Product / creator / scene / conversion path |
| Proof gallery | Captioned sample wall, not decorative collage |
| Campaign calendar | Product nodes and content rhythm |
| Budget | Procurement-style table with warm category accents |

## Reject Rules

- Generic supermarket stock image without proof label.
- Same image repeated across cards.
- Too many rounded cards with no hierarchy.
- Platform icons or logos copied without authorization.
- Beautiful content sample that does not say what asset or conversion it creates.
